This game was originally intended to have more "one way" passages
making it harder to navigate through the world.
Set this variable to true to enable this one way mode.
Some areas are not affected much, but I2 and I3 are significantly
affected. With this mode enabled, it is difficult to enter and exit
I2 or I3 without passing by White Box Devices (which take all your ammo).
For example, the only way in and out of Montreal requires going
by a White Box Device.  This mode also has fewer waypoints
in certain areas.  Use this at your own risk.
